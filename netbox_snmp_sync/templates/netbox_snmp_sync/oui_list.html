About OUI Lookup

OUI (Organisationally Unique Identifier) files map the first 3 octets of a MAC address to a vendor name.

After downloading, configure the plugin to use the files by setting oui_file in PLUGINS_CONFIG, e.g.:

"oui_file": [
  "/opt/netbox/oui/oui.csv",
  "/opt/netbox/oui/mam.csv",
]
